# Anti-patterns

Never:

- Invent employers, titles, dates, or tools
- Add skills from JD that are not in `resume.md`
- Inflate seniority (e.g. "led" without leadership evidence)
- Hide keywords in white text or metadata
- Paste JD paragraphs into the resume body
- Add unverifiable metrics
- Carry forward a skill from resume.md's Skills section into tailored-resume.md without checking it's backed by at least one bullet, stack line, or project elsewhere in resume.md
